Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

How do I get started with Ecolive?

Book a free 30-minute consultation. We'll discuss your space, timeline and budget, then prepare an initial design direction and transparent estimate.

What's the typical timeline for a project?

Most single-room projects complete in 4–8 weeks; full turnkey homes in 8–12 weeks depending on scope. You'll have a fixed schedule before we begin.

What's included in end-to-end service?

Everything — design, architectural layout, joinery and manufacturing, project management, installation and snag-free handover. One team, one point of contact.

Are designs fully customisable?

Yes. Every layout, material and finish is tailored to you. We start from your life and taste, never from a fixed catalogue.

What makes Ecolive sustainable?

FSC-certified timber, low-VOC finishes, durable materials chosen to last, and efficient in-house manufacturing that reduces waste and carbon.
