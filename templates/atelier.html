The studio

Luxury that costs the planet less.

Ecolive was founded on a simple belief: a beautiful home shouldn't come at the earth's expense. We specify responsibly-sourced timber, low-VOC finishes and durable materials made to last decades, not seasons — and we manage the whole build ourselves so nothing is lost in translation.

The result is interiors that feel quietly considered, age gracefully, and are genuinely yours.
